Top 3 Anime Of All Time

One Piece

This is by far the longest and the best ongoing anime in history.

Naruto

This is a wholesome anime where every villain changes his way of life and leaves villany after one speech from Naruto(the Protagonist).

Dragon Ball Series

This has some of the best action sequences and an engaging premise of the 90's anime and still makes one of the best fight sequences till this day.
